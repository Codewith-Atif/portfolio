ACTIONABLE FINDINGS

What management should do next

01

Stabilise night-shift quality

Night-shift defect rates trend above the portfolio average. Add first-piece inspection and targeted operator coaching.

02

Attack the downtime Pareto

Maintenance and yarn-break events create the largest avoidable capacity loss. Prioritise preventive maintenance on bottom-quartile lines.

03

Protect delivery reliability

Late orders cluster around low-efficiency production runs. Use a risk flag when efficiency falls below 88% or downtime exceeds 70 minutes.
